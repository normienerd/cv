About

Md. Sahariar Kabir Nion completed his bachelor's degree in civil engineering from the University of Asia Pacific (UAP), Dhaka. Currently, he is working remotely as a Research Assistant in the Azam Research Group at the Water-Energy-Food-Climate Nexus Lab, focusing on water and wastewater management. He is also working as a Fellow for Bangladesh Environment Network-BEN, where he is contributing to different BEN activities.

Previously, Nion interned as a Civil Engineer at Total Barakah Housing Bangladesh Limited-TBHBL, where he contributed to site supervision, project management, and cost estimation. He completed his undergraduate thesis on “Risk Assessment of Outfall Discharges into Turag River and Effect on Its Aquatic Ecosystems.” Beyond research, he actively participated in student leadership as President of the Structural Engineering Club at UAP Civil Engineering Department. His technical skill set includes ArcGIS Pro, IBM SPSS Statistics, Origin Pro, ETABS, and AutoCAD.
